Who We Are & The Problems We Solve

Eco-Mars is a youth-led green startup committed to solving two pressing environmental challenges: deforestation and waste paper accumulation.

Every year, millions of wooden pencils are produced, cutting down countless trees. Meanwhile, tons of paper waste pollute our planet. Plastic pens add to the problem with non-biodegradable waste.

We design eco-friendly pencils, color pencils, and plantable paper pens made from recycled paper. Each carries seeds ‚Äî so when the pencil ends, a new life begins: a plant, a flower, or even a tree.

Eco-Mars is more than stationery ‚Äî it‚Äôs a movement of sustainability.

Our Story

The story of Eco-Mars began in March 2023, when our founder, Roshaan Zahid, realized how deeply deforestation and waste were impacting our planet. As a student passionate about the environment, he observed that something as small as a pencil could be a hidden driver of climate problems.

he question was simple but powerful: Why should creativity and learning cost the Earth its trees?

With this thought, Roshaan and his close friends began experimenting with ways to replace wooden pencils with a more sustainable alternative. After months of trials, the first prototype of eco-friendly seed pencils was created in July 2023. That single prototype became the foundation of Eco-Mars.

The journey was not easy ‚Äî from learning about recycling processes to experimenting with strength, sharpening, and seed placement, every step demanded creativity and persistence. But with teamwork, determination, and a clear vision, Eco-Mars transformed from a small student project into a recognized national startup.

Today, Eco-Mars has scaled its products into eco-friendly pencils, color pencils, and pens, supplying them to schools, organizations, and exhibitions. What started in a small university workspace is now inspiring thousands of people to write, draw, and create with tools that protect the planet.

We design eco-friendly pencils, color pencils, and plantable paper pens made from recycled paper. Each carries seeds ‚Äî so when the pencil ends, a new life begins: a plant, a flower, or even a tree.

Our Mission

Our mission is: To reduce deforestation and paper waste by producing sustainable, plantable stationery that transform everyday actions into climate-positive impact.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every school, university office, and household uses eco-friendly stationery, replacing wooden pencils and plastic pens with greener alternatives. Our long-term vision is to make Eco-Mars a global leader in sustainable stationery, inspiring millions to take part in shaping a greener future.
